Privacy Policy

Effective date: 31.12.2025

This Privacy Policy explains how NovaApplications ("we", "us") collects and uses information when you use Cat Chase: Endless Run (the "App").

Summary

  • The App is child-directed and intended for a general audience including kids.
  • We do not use analytics.
  • We show ads through Google AdMob. Ads are non-personalized.
  • AdMob may collect the Android Advertising ID to serve and measure ads.
  • We collect crash reports to improve stability.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not require you to provide personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports. These reports may include device information (such as device model, OS version), app version, and crash logs.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to show ads. Because the App can be used by kids, we request non-personalized ads only and mark the app as child-directed and under age of consent.

Google AdMob may collect the Android Advertising ID to serve and measure ads.

AdMob may collect limited device information required to serve and measure ads. You can learn more here: Google Privacy Policy.

GDPR and Consent (EEA/UK/Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you may be shown a consent prompt provided by Google to comply with GDPR. We only serve non-personalized ads.

Consent revocation

In regions where consent is required (EEA, UK, and Switzerland), you can review or revoke your consent at any time in the App using the Privacy/Shield button on the start screen, which opens Google's privacy options form.

Children's Privacy

This App is designed for a general audience including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are requested as non-personalized.

Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to run the App: Google AdMob (ads) and Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
